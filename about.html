Who is teacher Ana?



Who?


Teacher Ana is a passionate educator who found her calling in teaching at a young age. As a teenager, she developed a deep love for history, inspiring her to pursue a degree in the subject with the dream of becoming a university professor. Education ran in her blood as her mother was a dedicated math teacher. After completing her studies, she embarked on an enriching journey, studying abroad in Canada. Upon returning to Brazil, destiny led her to discover a new path in teaching English at a language school. Over the years, she not only honed her teaching skills but also found love and companionship, meeting the person who would become her husband. However, life's challenges brought her to a turning point when she had to work long hours just to make ends meet. It was during this time that she discovered her true calling as a Tutor, leading her towards an exciting and fulfilling journey.



Why?


Teacher Ana's decision to become an educator was deeply rooted in her passion for sharing knowledge and empowering others. Her love for history drove her towards the path of teaching at the university level. However, life's circumstances led her to explore new opportunities in teaching English. Through this experience, she discovered the joy of helping students grasp a new language, building meaningful connections with them, and witnessing their growth and progress. Her dedication and commitment to her students became evident as she poured her heart and soul into every lesson. Teaching became more than just a profession; it became a calling, as she realized the impact she could have on shaping young minds and empowering them to achieve their dreams.



How?


As Teacher Ana prepared to take maternity leave after having her second daughter during a global pandemic, she needed to find capable teachers to replace her. This experience inspired her to mentor aspiring educators in private tutoring. Combining her own experiences and innovative ideas, she created the VHS method. This approach blended traditional and modern teaching techniques to help students reach their full potential while supporting the growth and success of teachers. The VHS method became a symbol of Teacher Ana's commitment to education, spreading the wonders of private tutoring to inspire teachers and students alike.
